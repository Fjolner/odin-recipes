Gullash Soup is often enjoyed in colder weather.

Its the perfect comfort food

Ingredients

  • Chopped beef
  • Potatoes
  • Chopped tomatoes
  • Carrots
  • Paprika
  • Sour cream

Steps

  1. Brown the minced meat
  2. Add the chopped tomatoes, potatoes, paprika, carrots and water
  3. Let simmer for 1 hour
  4. Serve while hot with sour cream
