Skipperlabskovs

Skipperlabskovs is a traditional dish in Denmark, which is

often made with leftover cowmeat from the day before.

It is served with rhybread.

Ingredients

  • chopped cowmeat
  • potatoes
  • butter
  • onions

Steps

  1. Melt the butter in a pot
  2. Put the meat, water and onion in the pan and cover with a lid
  3. Let is simmer for 1,5 hours
  4. Add the potatoes
  5. Let simmer lightly for 1 hour
  6. Serve while hot on rhybread
